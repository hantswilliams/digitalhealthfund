The FDA has provided a preliminary ML/Ai framework for health tech companies to potentially follow

In this article the Federal Drug Administration (FDA) outlines for the first time a working framework for healthcare startups to utilize when developing advanced statistical techniques (machine learning/artifical intelligence) for augmenting physician or care provider health delivery. Within this brief, the FDA defines ML, the concept of software as a medical device (SaMD), and a diagram that details 'Good Machine Learning Practices' on page 8, figure 2.

In summary, this articles highlights the existing gap - and continuous need for additional research and protocols related to the application of advanced statistical techniques like machine learning/artifical intelligence within the area of medicine and healthcare).
